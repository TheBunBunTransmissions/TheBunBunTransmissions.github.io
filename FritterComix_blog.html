Heres the veggie Pot pie recipe You guys have been asking for ^_^

1/2 cup of some sort of fat

2 cup of flour

1/2 cup water

2 eggs (Spuma or otherwise)

lil bit of salt

FILLING:

1 cup of milk (prefably Spuma , but alterintivly you can you spoink)

Salt (lil bit more than last time bout 2 pinches)

Garlic (2 cloves)

2 carrots

1/2 flour

1/3 cup of Peas

2 potatos (Mushed)

Seasoning of your choosing

__________

-make sure to spread the crust thin in order to get a nice crisp

-mix the filling in a pan first (on medium heat) befor baking
